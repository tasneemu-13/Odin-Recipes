Welcome To My Favourite Nigerian Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes/paste
  • Bell Peppers
  • Scotch Bonnet Peppers
  • Onions
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot, then saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add tomato paste and blended tomato and pepper mixture. Fry until the sauce thickens.
  3. Add thyme, curry, paprika, and ground pepper. Stir well.
  4. Pour in chicken or beef stock and bring to a boil.
  5. Add washed rice, stir, and reduce heat. Cover and let simmer until rice absorbs the liquid.
  6. Stir occasionally to prevent burning. Serve hot with fried plantains or grilled chicken.
